Our Mission

We work to create a safe, empowering space for girls and non-binary students in India to explore mathematics confidently. We recognize how gender bias and systemic barriers affect access to STEM for many students, especially non-binary youth. Our goal is to support young learners early—helping them discover joy in problem solving, grow into competitive thinkers, explore STEM pathways, and gain access to opportunities including olympiads, mentorship, and international programs.

Upcoming Events

INTEGIRLS Bangalore will host two free math competitions in the 2025–2026 school year: one in fall and one in spring. There will be three divisions: 6–8, 9–10, and 11–12. Top participants will earn exciting awards and may be selected for our Math Camp led by IMO/EGMO medalists and campers.

All girls, female-identifying, and non-binary teens across India may participate—no experience required! Sign-ups for the fall competition will open soon.
