Our Mission

       

          We work to create a safe, empowering space for girls and non-binary students in India to explore mathematics confidently.           We recognize how gender bias and systemic barriers affect access to STEM for many students, especially non-binary youth.           Our goal is to support young learners early—helping them discover joy in problem solving, grow into competitive thinkers,           explore STEM pathways, and gain access to opportunities including olympiads, mentorship, and international programs.        

     
   
       
     
       

Upcoming Events

       

          INTEGIRLS Bangalore will host two free math competitions in the 2025–2026 school year: one in fall and one in spring.           There will be three divisions: 6–8, 9–10, and 11–12. Top participants will earn exciting awards and may be selected for           our Math Camp led by IMO/EGMO medalists and campers.          

          All girls, female-identifying, and non-binary teens across India may participate—no experience required!           Sign-ups for the fall competition will open soon.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to participate in the competitions?

Our competitions are open to all girls, female-identifying, and non-binary teens across India who are currently in school up to grade 12. We offer three divisions (6–8, 9–10, and 11–12) to ensure a fair and engaging challenge for all experience levels. No prior competition experience is required!

What are the different divisions for the competitions?

We currently run three divisions:

  • **Middle School:** Grades 6, 7, and 8
  • **Junior Varsity:** Grades 9 and 10
  • **Varsity:** Grades 11 and 12
Participants should register for the division corresponding to their current grade level.

Is there a fee to participate in the INTEGIRLS Bangalore events?

No! All INTEGIRLS Bangalore competitions and workshops are completely **free** to participants. Our mission is to eliminate barriers to entry in STEM, and this includes financial costs.

How can I volunteer, mentor, or become a sponsor?

We are always looking for passionate volunteers and generous sponsors! Please visit our Sponsor Us section or contact our outreach team directly via the Contact page. Your support helps us provide free events and exciting prizes!
