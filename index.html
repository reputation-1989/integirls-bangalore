Our Mission

       

          We work to create a safe, empowering space for girls and non-binary students in India to explore mathematics confidently.           We recognize how gender bias and systemic barriers affect access to STEM for many students, especially non-binary youth.           Our goal is to support young learners early—helping them discover joy in problem solving, grow into competitive thinkers,           explore STEM pathways, and gain access to opportunities including olympiads, mentorship, and international programs.        

       

     
   
       
     
       

Upcoming Events

       

          INTEGIRLS Bangalore will host two free math competitions in the 2025–2026 school year: one in fall and one in spring.           There will be three divisions: 6–8, 9–10, and 11–12. Top participants will earn exciting awards and may be selected for           our Math Camp led by IMO/EGMO medalists and campers.          

          All girls, female-identifying, and non-binary teens across India may participate—no experience required!           Sign-ups for the fall competition will open soon.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

I’m interested but have no math competition experience. Can I still attend?

Of course, you should really attend this competition! In the past, many people have stated that INTEGIRLS gave them an entry into math competitions and they had no idea how fun it was until they attended. If you would like any pointers on how to prepare, please feel free to email us at bangalore@integirls.org. We hope to see you at our event(s)!

I do not have an INTEGIRLS Chapter near my area, but I am interested in participating. Can I still attend?

We encourage students not only from the Bangalore area but also from other regions in India and globally to attend! You can reach out to us using the contact methods provided at the bottom of this page (Contact Us) if you have any questions about remote participation.

Who can I contact if I have any questions?

You can contact us through Email or Instagram via the links provided at the bottom of this page. Please note that it may take up to **two weeks** to get a reply from us through email. If your query is **urgent**, you can message us on Instagram or add "URGENT" to the subject line of your email for faster response.

I do not identify as female or non-binary, but I would like to participate. How can I do that?

Our competitions are specifically for girls and non-binary students. If you do not identify as female or non-binary, we encourage you to check out other opportunities we may share, particularly through our social media channels. You can also contact bangalore@integirls.org to find more activities and resources that might be a good fit for you.

How can I contribute?

You can choose to **volunteer** for us by emailing us at bangalore@integirls.org. You can also **donate** to our non-profit here (link coming soon!). Alternatively, email us at the same address to receive more information about how you can help. **YOUR HELP MAKES A DIFFERENCE TO US!**

How many competitions will you conduct throughout the year?

We are currently **targeting to host two competitions a year**: one in the fall and one in the spring. More details about the specific dates and formats for these competitions will be available on this website soon, particularly in the Upcoming Events section.
